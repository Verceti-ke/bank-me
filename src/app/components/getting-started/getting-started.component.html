Getting Started

  1. CREATE a google spreadsheet and make it public by changing who can have access.

  2. INVITE bank-me-bot@bank-me-dev.iam.gserviceaccount.com as a user in your spreadsheet so that it can send your transactions to your sheet (with edit permission).

  3. COPY the google sheet id from the url bar (it is the longest random string). This is how the program will target your sheet as the destination. Save it until prompted for it.

  4. CONNECT your bank account and export your transactions from as far back as 24 months.
